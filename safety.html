Safety Disclaimer — GENEVIEVE App™ Food Safety V15

No-Guess Safety Mode is intentionally strict. If the app cannot prove a label is safe, it will not show green. OCR can misread labels and online product information can be outdated. Use the current physical label as the source of truth and seek medical care for ongoing diarrhoea, weight loss, dehydration, blood, fever, severe pain, fainting or inability to keep fluids down.
